About Luxinations

Luxinations is a gaming equipment store created to provide affordable and stylish gaming accessories. The website helps customers view products, compare options and place orders online.


Our target customers are gamers, students, content creators and technology users who need keyboards, mice, headsets, controllers and other gaming accessories.
